About Crane Convert

Crane Convert was developed by CoreLogic Studio to provide a simple, fast, and reliable tool for daily conversion needs. Our goal is to help users quickly convert between different units of measurement without unnecessary complexity or distractions.

The project started as a Chrome extension and has now expanded to include this web interface, making our conversion tools accessible to everyone, regardless of their browser or device.

We're constantly working to improve our tools and add new features. If you have any suggestions or feedback, please feel free to contact us.
